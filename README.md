# Money-Circle
Money Pool consists of 10 people that agree to contribute one fixed amount of money (100$) on a mentioned amount of time (1 month). When the group pools all the money, one member of this group will receive the funds. After a month, the same 10 people again will make their contribution and the money (1000$) is taken by another member in the circle. This continues till every member receive their lump sum of money.
